## Adding your own

Drop `.glb` files here and point a catalog entry at them in `src/main.js`
(`CATALOG.furn`, etc.), e.g. `robot:{ glb:'assets/models/robot.glb', scale:0.34, animate:'Dance' }`.
Prefer a single **CC0** art family (Kenney / Quaternius) for a cohesive look.
